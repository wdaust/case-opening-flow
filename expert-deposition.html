Expert Performance Index (EPI) — 0–100

Purpose: Composite score measuring expert quality, responsiveness, and reliability. Weighted across 6 components.
#ComponentWeightMeasures
1Retention Responsiveness20%Attempts needed to retain (fewer = higher score)
2Report Timeliness25%Days from materials to report vs. 21-day target
3Report Quality20%Amendment rate (lower = higher score)
4Amendment Turnaround10%Days to deliver amended report vs. 14-day target
5Deposition Performance15%Attorney rating of expert testimony strength
6Cost Efficiency10%Fee vs. market rate for specialty

EPI Bands

BandScoreLabelAction
Green80–100ElitePreferred expert list, priority retention
Yellow60–79SatisfactoryStandard workflow, monitor trends
Orange40–59UnderperformingPerformance review, consider alternatives
Red0–39CriticalDo-not-retain flag, management review

Risk Flags

Alert Triggers — Any of the following conditions generate an automatic risk flag:
  • Expert Unresponsive: 3 retention attempts with no response
  • Report Overdue: Expert report > 35 days past materials delivery
  • Amendment Loop: Same report amended 3+ times
  • Deposition Cancelled: Deposition cancelled < 48 hours before scheduled date
  • IME No-Show: Client fails to attend scheduled IME
  • Score Deterioration: Case score drops > 20 points post-deposition

Pressure Gap Indicator

Concept: Compares DPI (litigation pressure applied) against carrier/defense offer behavior. A high DPI with low offers signals defense resistance; a high DPI with rising offers signals effective pressure.
Pressure Gap = DPI Score − Normalized Offer Trend (0–100)
Positive gap: Pressure exceeding response (defense resisting)
Negative gap: Offers outpacing pressure (favorable negotiation dynamics)
Near zero: Pressure and response in equilibrium
Gap RangeSignalRecommended Action
< −10Offers exceeding pressureFavorable settlement window; consider accepting
−10 to +10EquilibriumContinue current strategy; monitor trends
+10 to +30Moderate resistanceIncrease deposition/expert activity; probe weak points
> +30Strong resistanceRe-evaluate case strategy; consider trial preparation

  2. 2
    Track Attempts
    On each attempt, increment BJB__Retain_Attempts__c and update BJB__Retain_Status__c.
  3. 3

  4. 4
