Expert Performance Index (EPI) — 0–100

Purpose: Composite score measuring expert quality, responsiveness, and reliability. Weighted across 6 components.
#ComponentWeightMeasures
1Retention Responsiveness20%Attempts needed to retain (fewer = higher score)
2Report Timeliness25%Days from materials to report vs. 21-day target
3Report Quality20%Amendment rate (lower = higher score)
4Amendment Turnaround10%Days to deliver amended report vs. 14-day target
5Deposition Performance15%Attorney rating of expert testimony strength
6Cost Efficiency10%Fee vs. market rate for specialty

EPI Bands

BandScoreLabelAction

Yellow60–79SatisfactoryStandard workflow, monitor trends
Orange40–59UnderperformingPerformance review, consider alternatives
Red0–39CriticalDo-not-retain flag, management review

Risk Flags

Alert Triggers — Any of the following conditions generate an automatic risk flag:
  • Expert Unresponsive: 3 retention attempts with no response
  • Report Overdue: Expert report > 35 days past materials delivery
  • Amendment Loop: Same report amended 3+ times
  • Deposition Cancelled: Deposition cancelled < 48 hours before scheduled date
  • IME No-Show: Client fails to attend scheduled IME
  • Score Deterioration: Case score drops > 20 points post-deposition

Pressure Gap Indicator

Concept: Compares DPI (litigation pressure applied) against carrier/defense offer behavior. A high DPI with low offers signals defense resistance; a high DPI with rising offers signals effective pressure.
Pressure Gap = DPI Score − Normalized Offer Trend (0–100)
Positive gap: Pressure exceeding response (defense resisting)
Negative gap: Offers outpacing pressure (favorable negotiation dynamics)
Near zero: Pressure and response in equilibrium
Gap RangeSignalRecommended Action
< −10Offers exceeding pressureFavorable settlement window; consider accepting
−10 to +10EquilibriumContinue current strategy; monitor trends
+10 to +30Moderate resistanceIncrease deposition/expert activity; probe weak points
> +30Strong resistanceRe-evaluate case strategy; consider trial preparation

Expert Management Pillar

Retention speed, report quality, disclosure compliance, expert performance scores

Measures the firm's ability to efficiently retain qualified experts, obtain high-quality reports, and meet all disclosure deadlines.

High: ≥90% on-time retention, ≥4.0 report quality, 100% disclosure compliance Low: <80% on-time, <3.0 quality, any missed disclosure deadlines

Scheduling efficiency, completion rates, transcript turnaround, testimony quality

Measures deposition workflow effectiveness across all deposition types: non-party, defendant, and client depositions.

High: ≥95% completion, ≥3.5 client depo score, on-time transcript review Low: <90% completion, <3.0 client score, transcript review delays

Defense Response Pillar

IME management, rebuttal filing, defense pressure tracking, strategic intelligence

Flow 1: Expert Retention Workflow

Trigger: Attorney creates expert directive (new Expert record with BJB__Retain_Status__c = 'Pending').
  1. 1
    Create Retention Task
    Auto-create task assigned to Para: "Retain Expert: [Name]". SLA = 1 business day.
  2. 2
    Track Attempts
    On each attempt, increment BJB__Retain_Attempts__c and update BJB__Retain_Status__c.
  3. 3
    Retention Success Path
    If retained: set status = "Retained", populate BJB__Retain_Date__c, auto-create "Send Materials" task.
  4. 4
    Retention Failure Path
    If 3 attempts exhausted: set status = "Failed", create "Replacement Directive" task for Attorney.

Flow 2: Report Follow-Up Automation

Trigger: BJB__Materials_Sent_Date__c is populated and BJB__Report_Status__c = 'Requested'.
  1. 1
    Schedule Follow-Up Tasks
    Auto-create follow-up tasks at Day 14, 21, 28, and 35 from materials sent date.
  2. 2
    Auto-Close on Receipt
    When BJB__Report_Received_Date__c is populated, auto-close remaining follow-up tasks.
  3. 3
    Escalation at Day 35
    If no report by Day 35, auto-notify management and flag case for intervention.

Flow 3: IME Reminder Automation

Trigger: BJB__IME_Date__c is populated and BJB__IME_Notice_Sent__c = true.
  1. 1
    10-Day Reminder
    Auto-send client reminder 10 days before IME with date, time, and location confirmation.
  2. 2
    1-Day Reminder
    Auto-send final reminder 1 day before with directions and contact info.

Flow 4: Deposition Scheduling

Trigger: New Deposition record created with BJB__Depo_Status__c = 'Scheduled'.
  1. 1
    Notice Prep Task
    Auto-create task: "Prepare Depo Notice for [Witness]". SLA = 5 business days.
  2. 2
    Verification Task
    Auto-create verification task due 5 days before depo date.
  3. 3
    Post-Depo Tasks
    On completion, auto-create: "Order Transcript" and "Score Case" tasks.

Flow 5: SLA Enforcement Engine

Trigger: Scheduled process runs daily at 8:00 AM.
  1. 1
    Scan Open Tasks
    Query all open tasks where due date has passed or is approaching.
  2. 2
    Apply Escalation Ladder
    Based on days overdue, apply appropriate escalation: Para lead → Manager → Attorney → Management.
  3. 3
    Generate Notifications
    Send email/Litify notifications to appropriate escalation targets.

Flow 6: Score Calculation Engine

Trigger: Scheduled weekly batch + on-demand when key fields update (report received, depo scored, etc.).
  1. 1
    Calculate EPI
    For each expert: weight 6 components per formula, store in BJB__EPI_Score__c.
  2. 2
    Calculate DPI
    For each case: weight 9 components, store in BJB__DPI_Score__c.
  3. 3
    Calculate ELI & TRI
    Compute Expert Leverage and Trial Readiness indices per formulas.
  4. 4
    Compute Combined Strategic Score
    Apply combined formula: (DPI×0.25)+(ELI×0.20)+(TRI×0.20)+(CPI×0.20)+(CSI×0.15).

⚠ Risk Flags (6 Triggers)

⌄
Purpose: Automated risk flags that trigger management alerts and workflow interventions when critical thresholds are breached.
  1. Expert Report Overdue >7 Days: Report not received within 7 days of deadline. Triggers replacement expert evaluation.
  2. Expert Daubert Challenge Filed: Defense files Daubert/Frye motion against retained expert. Triggers immediate attorney review and rebuttal strategy.
  3. Client Deposition Score <2.5: Client performed poorly at deposition. Triggers case strategy reassessment and settlement evaluation.
  4. IME No-Show: Client failed to attend scheduled IME. Triggers immediate outreach, rescheduling, and potential sanctions mitigation.
  5. Adverse IME Report Without Rebuttal: IME report received with adverse findings and no rebuttal initiated within 14 days. Triggers attorney escalation.
  6. Disclosure Deadline <7 Days, Report Missing: Expert disclosure deadline approaching with outstanding reports. Triggers emergency pursuit and extension evaluation.
